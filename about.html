About PPSU

P P Savani University (PPSU) sits ensconced among one of the most scenic campuses in this part of the country. It is spread across a green expanse covering 100 acres housing the Schools of Agriculture, Design, Engineering, Homeopathy, Liberal Arts, Specialized Sciences, Management, Nursing, and Physiotherapy. The School of Ayurveda will add its name to the assemblage soon.

PPSU inherits a glorious legacy of the P P Savani Group whose founder, the Edupreneur, Shri Vallabh Savani holds the meritorious distinction of changing the education landscape in South Gujarat. The Group under his stewardship has nurtured and groomed more than a million students through their network of educational institutions.

The ever-changing spectrum of skills and specialization requires the student of today to be in sync with the needs of tomorrow. P P Savani University with its undergraduate, postgraduate, research, certificate, and skill-development programmes coupled with a cutting-edge infrastructure offers students a platform to pursue their dreams by equipping them with skills and knowledge needed in the 21st century's demanding workplaces.
